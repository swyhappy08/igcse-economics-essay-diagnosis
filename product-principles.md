# Product Principles

- Diagnose before teaching.
- Evidence over intuition.
- Mechanism over keywords.
- Improve through real student writing.
- Human-reviewed before prompt updates.

The goal is not to replace teachers. The goal is to make high-quality diagnosis accessible before teaching begins.

These aren't features — they're the same principles this project's marking approach already follows in real tutoring. The AI system is a translation of an existing teaching approach into something students can use directly, not a replacement for it.
